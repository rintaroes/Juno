# Juno: Business + Design Notes

## Business Aspect
- **Core value:** Help users quickly organize and act on personal information in one mobile workspace.
- **Target users:** Busy professionals, students, and creators who need lightweight daily planning.
- **Monetization direction:** Freemium model (free core, paid advanced workflows and integrations).
- **Growth loop:** Useful daily utility -> repeat usage -> referrals through shared workflows/templates.

## Design Notes
- Keep UI minimal and task-first; reduce taps to complete common actions.
- Use clear visual hierarchy for priorities, deadlines, and next actions.
- Favor calm, neutral colors with one strong accent for key CTAs.
- Ensure fast loading and smooth interactions to reinforce trust and daily habit.
